This project demonstrates the design and querying of a relational database simulating a retail shoe store. The database models business
entities such as customers, orders, and information on the products.

The project includes: 
* Designing normalized tables with primary and foreign key relationships
* Importing and structuring sales data for transactions
* Writing SQL queries to analyze business performances

Key analysis performed:
* Total revenue and sales over time
* Customer spending behavior
* Popular products and purchasing trends/patterns
